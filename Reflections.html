REFLECTIONS-22

 
General Rules

 • The events marked (i) are individual events and that marked (g) are group events.
 • Timing will be strictly followed as per the schedule.
 • The school authorities are requested to hand over the Registration form and the overall
   registration fees with the names of the participants only to the organizers who will personally
   come to the school to collect the above with theRotaract identity.
 • Every school must be represented by a teacher as the manager of the school team. In case 
   of any dispute only the manager/Principal has the 
   authority to question the organizers. Any feedback from the parents is to be informed through 
   their particular school representatives or managers.
 • All the participants must register within the time mentioned in the schedule.
 • It is the responsibility of the manager to register the students during the registration process.
 • Each school shall be given a slot number for the fair and smooth conduction of the event.
 • It is the responsibility of the manager to make sure the participant is present at the right time 
   for an event.
 • Prizes will be distributed in the valediction, at the end of the second day by eminent personalities.
 • Kindly note that the students willing to participate in more than one event do not select the events 
   with overlapping time.
 • Students and the manager of the school are requested to note the scheduling of all the events at the 
   time of registration to prevent any kind of confusion.
 • Registration for group events after the submission of the participation list will not be entertained.
